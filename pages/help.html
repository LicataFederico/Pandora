IMPORTANT INFORMATION

To return one or more items from your order, please follow the below-mentioned procedure:

1) For a simple return, please use the pre-printed return form and return label included in your parcel. Please fill in the return form provided in the packaging.

2) Make sure all products you wish to return and all the forms, product tags, authenticity labels or cards are included in the package, the original box or an equally robust box. Attach the return label to the outside of the parcel. If you cannot find the return label, please contact our Customer Care.

3) Contact the UPS number that you have or take the package to the nearest UPS point. We recommend that you keep an eye on the tracking that you find on the return label placed inside the package, so that you can monitor the shipment of your package.

Any issues caused by the use of a courier or a return label other than ours are not attributable to Pandora.

Your return may take until 5 business days to be handled by our warehouse. After that you will receive a confirmation email. The refund will be processed to the credit/debit card or account originally used for payment. Refunds may take up to 10 working days to appear on your bank statement, depending on your bank.

Please note that if you have received a gift and would like to return it for a refund, the person who originally purchased the gift will receive the refund. We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

Currently, it is not possible to return the items to a Pandora Retail Store.

You will find the dispatch options as well as the delivery costs and times in the following table. For Second Day Shipping and Next Day Shipping the order must be placed by 11 am EST in order to be shipped same day. Please note that all orders are subject to method of payment verification, and if we are unable to verify your purchase in time this can delay your shipment. Orders are usually confirmed, packaged and dispatched in 1-2 business days after the order is placed.
